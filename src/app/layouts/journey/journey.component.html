My Journey in the World of IT

I've been fascinated by the world of IT since I was a kid.
I was first introduced to computers back in the Windows 95 era, and I dreamed of creating my own games one day. Life took me in a different direction, leading me to study Accounting and Management instead. But I never gave up on my passion — and eventually, I found my way back, building a career in the IT field I've always loved.

January 2020
The beginning of my journey at the Directorate of Information Systems and Treasury Technology, joining the SAKTI team.
November 2020
I was trusted to become a developer for one of DJPb’s core applications, SAKTI. I received a GitLab account, even though I hadn’t yet learned any programming languages.
SAKTI Frontend Developer
December 2020
I started learning programming using PHP and the CodeIgniter framework. I built a Point of Sales (POS) system for my wife’s cookie business.
March 2021
My very first commit to SAKTI! Although it came with a defect the next day.
September 2021
I was given my own development branch to revamp the entire SAKTI frontend UI to a more modern design (SAKTI UI Redesign).
January 2022
I began upskilling by learning the Java-based backend system of SAKTI, evolving into a Full Stack Developer.
SAKTI Full Stack Developer
March 2023
I was assigned a new task to improve the homepage of the Monsakti application.
Monsakti Frontend Developer
September 2023
I was reassigned to a non-IT unit, but I never gave up learning IT. I planned to build a game using Godot and developed innovations for my new unit.
Freelance Developer - Kantin Treasury & Ikuchecker
September 2024
I was reassigned to the IT unit to develop an integration platform. I was trusted to redesign the frontend based on the mockups from the design team.
MyIntress Full Stack Developer
July 2025
I was assigned to fix and improve the frontend of a new internal application called Impact.
Impact Frontend Developer
Now
“Learning never stops.
As a developer, I’m committed to growing every single day.”
